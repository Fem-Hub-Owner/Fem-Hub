Want to Join the Team?

Become a Moderator! 🌟

Are you passionate about helping others and keeping Fem Hub awesome? We're always looking for new moderators to join our team!

What Moderators Do: Help keep the community safe and welcoming, answer questions and help new members, create and organize events, resolve conflicts respectfully, and represent our values of inclusivity and support.

What We're Looking For: Active, kind, and responsible community members. Someone who embodies Fem Hub values. Good communication skills. Patience and empathy. Commitment to making the community better.

Interested? Contact us to learn more about becoming a moderator! We'd love to have you on the team.

Volunteer Opportunities 💜

Even if you don't want to be a moderator, there are tons of ways to help! You can organize a local meetup, write blog posts, contribute to our gallery, help new members, answer FAQ questions, or just spread positivity in the community. Every contribution makes a difference!
