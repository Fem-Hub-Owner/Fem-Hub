Want to Organize a Meetup?

Start Your Own Meetup! 🎉

Is there no meetup in your area? No problem! Organize one yourself! Here's how:

Step 1: Think of a fun activity (shopping, coffee, movies, parks, events, etc.)

Step 2: Choose a date, time, and location that works for you

Step 3: Create a meetup post on Fem Hub describing what you're planning

Step 4: Spread the word! Invite community members from your area

Step 5: Have an amazing time with your new friends!

📋 Meetup Guidelines

Safety First: Meet in public places. Let a friend know where you're going. Trust your gut.

Be Respectful: Everyone deserves kindness and respect. Treat others how you'd want to be treated.

Inclusivity: Welcome everyone! All experience levels, all styles, all backgrounds. Fem Hub is for everyone.

Have Fun: The goal is to make friends and create memories. Keep it light, positive, and fun!

No Pressure: It's totally cool if you can't make a meetup. No judgment. Show up when you can!

💬 Meetup Ideas

Not sure what to plan? Here are some popular meetup ideas: Shopping days at thrift stores or malls, Coffee or brunch hangouts, Movie nights, Park picnics, Karaoke nights, Art galleries or museum visits, Pride events, Gaming or board game nights, Photography walks, Fashion shows or style challenges, Dinner dates, Beach or outdoor adventures!
